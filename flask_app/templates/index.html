Data-driven SAR Operations & Decision Making

Search and Rescue (SAR) & SARSAT Data

Applying data analysis and machine learning, we provide results that have immediate and tangible impacts to operations. 


False alerts are a waste of valuable resources. Our tools identify actual alerts and deprioritize likely false alerts in real time, enhancing decision-making and preventing the waste of search and rescue resources. 


We automate and integrate checklists, data-entry, and standard procedures - to improve work-flow, save time, and ensure optimal results. 


Search and rescue is complex enough. Our unique approach using existing tools and processes directly supports individual RCCs. No need for lengthy approval processes for software or bureaucratic roadblocks for large-scale procurements.

Operationalized Results

Results are in the form of a desktop python based tool. When a SARSAT alert is received, another pop-up opens, with an assessment if the notification is actually distress or a false alert, and the rational for that recommendation.  This allows watch standers and operators to focus on what they do best - respond to actual search and rescue emergencies.
